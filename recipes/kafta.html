Kafta

Description

Kafta is one of the most popular middle eastern meat to bring to the cookout. This delicious preparation for ground beef results in a delicious meat mixture that can be used for meatballs, barbeque skewers, and barbeque hamburgers!

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Parsley
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Lebanese 7 Spice
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, grated onion, parsley, cayenne, Lebanese 7 spice, salt, and pepper until evenly blended.
  3. Shape ground beef into skewers and grill for 10 to 15 minutes until meat is no longer pink.
