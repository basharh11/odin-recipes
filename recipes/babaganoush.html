Baba Ganoush

Description

Baba Ganoush is another popular appetizer in the middle east. If you want to make this delicious roasted eggplant dip with an irresistible smoky flavor to enjoy at home, we've got you covered!

Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • Olive Oil
  • Parsley
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Tahini
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Prepare a grill for medium-high heat. Grill the eggplant, rotating it occasionally, until it is soft.
  2. Steam the eggplants to allow the residual liquid to drain by placing it in a bowl covered with plastic wrap.
  3. Chop the eggplants in half and scoop out the flesh.
  4. Place the flesh in a bowl with the tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and salt.
  5. Hand mix until it's well combined with a slightly chunky texture.
  6. Add a drizzle of olive oil and add some freshly chopped parsley.
