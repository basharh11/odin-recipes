Hummus

Description

Hummus is undoubtedly the most popular appetizer in the middle east. If you want to make this delicious and creamy dip to enjoy at home, we've got you covered!

Ingredients

  • Canned Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemons
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Garnish

Steps

  1. Drain the canned chickpeas in a colander and keep the liquid handy.
  2. Add the chickpeas, chickpea liquid, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin and salt to the blender
  3. Turn blender on high for 30 seconds.
  4. Add more chickpea liquid to change the consistency to your liking.
  5. Create a divot with the back of the spoon for the olive oil to sit in.
  6. Sprinkle some paprika and add some freshly chopped parsley.
